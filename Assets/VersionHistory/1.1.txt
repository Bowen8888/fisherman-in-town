New Features:

	Settings panel.
	You can now change game timer mode to automatic in settings. 
	Tutorials are added, which can be found in settings as well.
	Status color will change to red when status is low.

	Music and sound controls are moved to settings.
	Footer can now be seen in food panel.
	Foods are sorted by price.

Bug fixes:

	Investment related bugs are fixed.
	Leader-board money update bug fixed.
